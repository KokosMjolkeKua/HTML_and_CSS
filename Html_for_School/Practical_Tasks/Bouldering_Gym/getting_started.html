Getting Started with Bouldering

How to get started

Unlike many outdoor sports, getting started with bouldering is surprisingly simple.
All you need are some climbing shoes, chalk, and a positive attitude—most gyms even rent the equipment for your first sessions.
No prior experience or complex gear required!

Simple steps to getting started

  1. Find a bouldering gym near you.
  2. Rent or invest in a pair of climbing shoes.
  3. Learn basic bouldering techniques.
  4. Start with easy routes and gradually increase difficulty.
  5. Join a bouldering community or club.
