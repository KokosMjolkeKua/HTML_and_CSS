About the Raccoon

Adorable masked bandit of the wild

Raccoons are medium-sized mammals known for their distinctive black "mask" markings around their eyes and bushy, ringed tails. They are highly adaptable and can live in forests, urban areas, and anywhere with food sources.

In some regions, raccoons are considered invasive and can impact local ecosystems and spread diseases.
