About the Goldfish

The popular ornamental fish

Goldfish are freshwater fish commonly kept as pets. They were domesticated in China over a thousand years ago. Known for their bright orange, gold, or white colors, they are relatively easy to care for but need a clean and spacious environment.

Goldfish can live for many years with proper care and have been bred into many different varieties.

Diet

  • Flake food
  • Pellets
  • Vegetables (peas, lettuce)
  • Live or frozen food (brine shrimp, bloodworms)
